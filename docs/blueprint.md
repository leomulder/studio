# **App Name**: Paroles Claires

## Core Features:

- Guided Bible Study: Provides verse-by-verse explanations of biblical texts from Patriarchs and Prophets.
- Multi-Device Access: Allows users to access the study material on multiple devices, ensuring a seamless experience.
- Personal Note-Taking: Enables users to take personal notes within the app to record their reflections and insights.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft beige (#F5F5DC) to create a calm and serious tone, evoking a sense of historical authenticity.
- Background color: Light grey (#F0F0F0), almost white, for a clean and breathable feel, enhancing readability.
- Accent color: Muted brown (#A69079), to highlight key elements and CTAs subtly without overwhelming the user.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern serif with high contrast, gives an elegant and fashionable feel, which will be paired with 'PT Sans'.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) will pair with 'Playfair' and ensure readability in longer text sections, lending warmth and personality to the clean design.
- Use simple and consistent line icons throughout the landing page to visually represent key concepts and benefits.
- Maintain a clean, spacious layout with ample whitespace to ensure the page is easy to scan and feels uncluttered. Avoid aggressive marketing elements.
